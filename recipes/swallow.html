The Swallow Combos

Amala and Ewedu recipes

Description

Ingredients

For the Amala:

  • Elubo flour

For the Ewedu soup:

  • Ewedu leaf (Jute leaves)
  • Iru (Locust bean)
  • Small pinch of salt

Lastly for the Stew

  • Blended Tomatoes + Onions + Rodo (Hot pepper) + Scotch bonnet
  • Crayfish
  • Fish (smoke or fried)
  • Kpomo (Cow skin)
  • Palm oil
  • Seasoning
  • Iru (Locust bean) for more flavor
  • Dry Spices: Blended powder of Ginger, Nutmeg, Cloves.

Direction

For the Amala

  1. Pour water in a pot and boil,
  2. After boiling the water while still on the gas, start pouring your flour and turn/stir quickly to prevent lumbs.
  3. Turn till it smooth and desried consistency is attained.
  4. Package into kitchen wraps till ready for use.

For the Ewedu

  1. Wash and blend the leaves. (Some normally pound or whisk it.)
  2. Pour water in pot and boil(small amout of water to avoid losing it thickness)
  3. Add salt and iru and the ewedu
  4. Whisk and allow to boil for couple of minutes and off the heat.

For the assorted stew

  1. First boil your kpomo with little seasoning till soft then,
  2. Pour palm oil in pot, let it hot a bit to fry your tomatoes blends. Add iru and cryfish as you wait till it dry.
  3. Add kpomo and the fish
  4. Add seasoning and spices
  5. allow to boil for a while.
  6. Then add the dry or smoke fish, allow for couple of minutes and off the heat.
  7. Now, serve as shown in the picture.

Note: Ewedu is prepared almost plain but ganishes with the assorted stew.
