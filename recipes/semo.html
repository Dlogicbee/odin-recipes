The Swallow Combos

Efo Riro with Semo

Description

Ingredients

For the Semo:

  • Any kind of semo flour

For the Efo Riro:

  • 1 bunch of Efo leaves (spinach leaves)
  • Blended Fresh Tatashe & Pepper
  • 1 tbsp of Iru (Locust beans)
  • 4 tbsp Palm oil
  • 1 big chopped Onions
  • Seasonings
  • Â½ cup of grounded Crayfish
  • Smoke Fish
  • 200 gram Beef
  • 100 gram Assorted Meat

Directions

For the Semo:

  1. Pour water in pot and boil it,
  2. Mix little Semo flour in a small bowl and mix it with half cup of water.
  3. First, add the mixed flour to the hot water while on the cooking gas,
  4. Then add Semo flour. Stir it immediately to avoid lumbs in it.
  5. For best taste, add a little water to it and cover the pot for some minutes and allow it to cook.
  6. Stir again for moderation and serve.

For the Efo Riro

  1. Wash and boil the assorted meat with seasonings, salt, and onions. (10-15 minutes)
  2. Add beef to it and cook till tender for another 10 minutes.
  3. Add fish to it, boil for 5 minutes. Ensure only a little water is left in the pot. Then remove from heat and set it aside
  4. Now, use another pot to heat up some palm oil.
  5. Then add the chopped onions to fry till it turn gold and add the blended pepper and tatashe. Stir fry for about 5-7 minutes. Avoid burning while stirring occasionally.
  6. Add the cooked meat, beef and fish, gently stir together. Cover to boil for 5 minutes.
  7. Add the crayfish, locust beans, then leave to boil for about 3-4 minutes
  8. Add seasonings, salt, to taste well. Stir together carefully.
  9. Add the chopped Efo leaves (spinach). Stir and leave to boil under low heat for 5 minutes.
  10. Remove from heat. Now ready to serve.
